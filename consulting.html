Consulting

  

If you are looking for consulting, research, or development services, get in touch immediately by writing to hello@datakolektiv.com about your needs. Ali inquiries will be processed and answered in no more than 24h. Thank you.

What is in the store

We are specialized in and have an extensive, immediate experience in the solving any of the following problems:

  • Predictive Analytics: we provide predictive analytics models in various business contexts and use cases;
  • Exploratory Analytics: understanding the most important “why” question from your data and business processes;
  • Market/Business Analytics: customer segmentation, survey data analysis, churn prediction, choice analysis/Generalized Linear Models, behavioral choice under risk and uncertainty;
  • Social Media Analytics: scoring, user segmentation, network analysis;
  • Information Retrieval: NLP, recommendation engines, knowledge extraction, automatic semantic tagging, search engines, embedding models, topic models.

We never go for off-the-shelf solutions immediately. Every new business problem is assessed as a unique case and the choice of the appropriate tech stack is suggested along the lines of that initial analysis.

Roadmap

  • In a typical context, we would first ask to perform a pilot study and develop a prototype of the solution; pricing in this phase is flexible and depends upon the particulary details of the business problem under consideration.
  • This can be done with a limited amount of data and always includes an introductory workshop with the client.
  • If the the pilot study shows that a feasible, approximate solution to the problem is possible, and if the following discussion with the client reaches an agreement that the prospects for a full-scale solution are good,
  • only then we will propose a strategy roadmap to the client including the description of all development phases, the planned tech stack, the timeline, and the specific related costs.
